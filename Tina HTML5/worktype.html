The Different Drummer

The Different Drummer spearheads alternative paths to success using creativity and imagination.

Who You Are

Some people are willing to think outside the box. You? You want to blow that mother up. You take open-mindedness to a whole new level, absolutely embracing new experiences. More than most people, you're intellectually curious and appreciative of the beauty around you. This applies to your general personality, as well as the way you approach your work. When faced with a new task, you address it with creativity and imagination, looking for interesting ways to tackle the problem.

When it comes to conventional thinking and following traditions and customs, you're just not interested. You greatly prefer to chart new territory, see in new ways, and use your hands and your mind to create new things. As a result, you're most likely highly prized as a thinker, as someone who's able to come up with inventive and insightful responses to new issues as they arrive.

Even though you thrive in unconventional, unstructured settings, keep in mind that not everyone does. Your coworkers, and even your manager, may approach their work much differently from how you do. So continue to bring your enthusiasm and imagination to your job, but watch for ways, as well, to appreciate other approaches, and to integrate them into your own way of doing things.

As an expressive and innovative artist, you take pride in bringing abstract ideas to life. You want to work on the conceptual stages of a project in a free and unstructured environment. Being confined by rigid guidelines and a complete lack of autonomy is your worst nightmare. Toiling away on set routines with no creative outlets just seems like it would be a soul crushing waste of your talents. Try to recognize and appreciate that routine-oriented workers are ultimately the people who bring your artistic visions to life.

How You Work In Teams

Looks like you’re ready to take on the one-of-a-kind challenges that scare away your peers. If traditional techniques are getting you nowhere, you’re not afraid to abandon them and forge your own path to success.

Your peers will respect your knack for inventing and implementing original strategies.

In fact, your openness will inspire your teammates to become more resourceful themselves. Roadblocks and unexpected problems don’t frustrate you. They just provide you with another opportunity to excel.

This sense of imagination and creativity helps you with more than just leading teams. It also helps you in day-to-day life. You’re willing to take risks and go where no one has gone before. Once in a while one of your strategies may fail, but that hardly matters since you’ll be eager to pick up the pieces and try again.

Your Strengths

  • You’re not afraid to take risks and seek out challenges.
  • You can take charge when unexpected issues or problems arise and lead your team to success.
  • You should avoid repetitive projects with established and restricting procedures.
